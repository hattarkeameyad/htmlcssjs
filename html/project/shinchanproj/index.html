Shin-chan (Shinnosuke Nohara) is a mischievous, five-year-old Japanese boy famous for his cheeky behavior, love of beautiful women, and signature "butt dance," starring in the long-running Crayon Shin-chan manga and anime series about his daily life with his parents , little sister Himawari , and pet dog Shiro, known for creating chaos at his kindergarten
